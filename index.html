Basic Plan


Price: R150-R300/Month


Ideal for: Beginners or casual gym-goers


Includes:


  • Access to gym equipment (cardio + weights)
  • Limited access hours (e.g., off-peak)
  • No guest privileges
  • No group classes
  • No pool/sauna/spa

Standard Plan


Price: R350-R700/Month


Ideal for: Regular users who want some extra features


Includes:


  • Full access to gym equipment during all hours
  • Group fitness classes (e.g., Zumba, yoga, spin)
  • Access to multiple locations (for some chains)
  • Basic locker room access
  • Occasional guest passes
